Biography

My name is Efekomwan Anthony Oriakhi, born on December 03, in Lagos State, Nigeria. I grew up in a bustling household as the fourth of six children. My parents instilled in me the values of hard work, perseverance, and the importance of family. Growing up in Lagos, a city known for its vibrant culture and dynamic lifestyle, and Benin City, one of the oldest Kingdoms with a rich cultural heritage, I developed a keen sense of adventure and curiosity about the world around me.

Education

I attended high school in Benin City, where I received a solid education that laid the foundation for my future endeavors. Despite the challenges that often accompany a large family, I remained dedicated to my studies and demonstrated a strong interest in learning and exploring new ideas.

Career

After completing my high school education, I embarked on a career that combined my love for travel and my entrepreneurial spirit. I became a self-employed travel consultant, a role that allowed me to help others achieve their dreams of exploring the world. With my extensive knowledge of travel logistics and destinations, I successfully guided numerous clients on their journeys to Europe, Asia, and America. My dedication to providing excellent service earned me a reputation as a reliable and knowledgeable consultant in the travel industry.

Achievements

My achievements as a travel consultant are noteworthy. Through my expertise, I have facilitated countless successful trips for my clients, helping them navigate the complexities of international travel. My ability to tailor travel plans to meet individual needs and preferences has made me a trusted advisor to many. My work not only brought joy and new experiences to my clients but also contributed to the growth and success of my business.

Personal Interests

Outside of my professional life, I am driven by a passion for continuous learning and personal growth. I have a deep-seated interest in visiting new places and acquiring new skills. Whether it's exploring a new city, learning a new language, or mastering a new technology, I approach every opportunity with enthusiasm and a desire to expand my horizons. I believe that the knowledge and skills I gain can be applied to solve everyday problems, ultimately generating income and enhancing the quality of life. I have been self-employed as a travel consultant for several years and have some familiarity with using computers due to my nature of business. I am also good at arts and crafts and have a desire to learn new things. Despite my educational background and the time wasted, I have made several attempts, such as the CS50 Harvard University free online computer course, which I started but stopped at the scratch level using Scratch to build games ten years ago. I applied for the Microverse online course when it was free three years ago, where I learned how to install and use two systems on my computer: Windows and Linux "Ubuntu." However, I stopped because I couldn't rectify a bug that I knew would reflect in my portfolio if I continued.

Goals

My journey is a testament to my resilience, curiosity, and dedication. I have built a successful career on my own terms, driven by my passion for travel and my commitment to helping others. My story serves as an inspiration to those who seek to combine their interests with their entrepreneurial aspirations. As I continue to explore the world and acquire new skills, I remain focused on using my experiences to create value and solve problems.

My goal is to keep learning, growing, and making a positive impact on my community and the world at large. At the end of the program with AltSchool Africa, I aim to acquire new skills in Frontend engineering, upgrade myself, get certified, and grow in software development and beyond. After getting certified, my desire is to use what I have learned to develop innovative solutions. I aspire to become a go-to expert in the field, solving complex problems, contributing meaningfully to the tech industry, and upgrading myself with critical thinking to solve everyday problems.
